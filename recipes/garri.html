Garri

A soaked garri with some ice, NOTHING BEATS IT!

Garri is one of the top classic Nigerian dishes widely popular in every local household, regardless of state, region or ethnicity.

Garri is made of peeled and grounded cassava

Ingredients

  • garri
  • ice cubes
  • water
  • sugar
  • milk
  • groundnuts

Steps

  1. Get a bowl
  2. Add preferred quantity of garri into the bowl
  3. Add sugar, milk and groundnuts
  4. Add water and ice cubes
  5. Voila, your meal is ready, get a spoon!
